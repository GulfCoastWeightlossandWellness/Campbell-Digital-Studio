## The case study

### The challenge
ACExperts started from nothing — new business, new domain, no search history, no reviews, no rankings. In Baldwin County HVAC, the established names have 25 to 40 years of trust and hundreds of reviews. The site's job was to make a brand-new company findable and credible fast enough to compete before it had any of that — and to turn that visibility into booked work, not just traffic. [verified: brand-new domain, GSC history starts 4/22/2026]

### What I built
A full-stack Next.js platform now live at acexperts251.com — **274 pages** [verified: live sitemap]: a programmatic city × service matrix covering **17 Baldwin County cities** [verified] (171 service-area pages), dedicated service hubs, a native blog engine running 62+ posts, and interactive diagnostic tools no competitor in the county offers. Every city × service intersection is its own indexed, internally linked page with unique local content and schema — the same programmatic pattern enterprise sites use, applied to one county. ### What happened — the first seven weeks
This is the part that matters. From a standing start on 4/22:

**Indexing.** 215 pages indexed on Google — about 78% of the sitemap — with only 7 pages quality-rejected across the whole site. The rest of the not-yet-indexed is simply crawl queue. [verified: GSC, 2026-06-13]

**Visibility.** ~44,800 Google impressions in the first seven weeks. The ramp tells the story — near-invisible for two weeks while Google crawled, then 1,700 → 6,600 → ~15,500 impressions per week by weeks six and seven, now holding ~1,800–2,200 a day. [verified: GSC, daily data 4/22–6/11]

**The local map pack.** Within seven weeks, ranking #1 in the local pack for "AC repair" in Robertsdale, Lillian, Stockton, and Rosinton, and top-three in Silverhill and Summerdale — against incumbents with 25–40 years and hundreds of reviews. [verified: signed-in manager check 6/13; confirm against your incognito checks]

**Google Business Profile.** 304 profile views and 78 customer actions — calls, directions, clicks — in the same window. The searches surfacing the profile: "ac repair near me," "ac repair robertsdale," "acexperts." [verified: GBP dashboard, Mar–Jun]

**The money.** A website form lead became an **$8,500 system install** — and that job helped push the business past **~$30,000 in revenue** in its first seven weeks. [owner: Landon, confirmed in writing 6/13]

### Why it matters
Most agencies show you a build. This shows the chain: a brand-new business went from zero to indexed, visible, ranking, and booking real work in under two months — and one lead off the site paid for the site many times over. The website and the Google Business Profile run as one system: 43 organic clicks understate it badly, because the GBP added 78 more customer actions and the form turned a lead into $8,500. That's what a CDS build is for.
